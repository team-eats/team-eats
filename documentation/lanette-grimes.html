Attitudes and Needs

Why are they here?

Lanette loves going out to eat but has a few allergies. She wants to know where she can find a restaurant that she can be sure will have something for her.

What needs do they have that we cater to?

She needs to ebe able to sort through the restaurants around town efficiently without checking every menu.

Why us over other options?

Our platform allows users and business owners to add food tags, such as "Vegan", "Chinese", "New Mexican", "Gluten Free", to a restaurant's profile which can help discoverability.

What frustrates them?

Keeping a mental list of places she can eat and regularly checking menus for updates.

Wanting to try eating somewhere new but having to sort through reviews or manually checking websites for compatible food options.

A restaurant her friends want to eat at doesn't have a website.

  5. The Vietnamese checkbox gets filled in.
  6. She scrolls on the menu to the allergy section.
  7. The menu moves through sections.
  8. Lanette taps the dairy allergy box.
  9. The dairy allergy box gets filled in.
