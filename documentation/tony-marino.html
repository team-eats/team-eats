Brick and Mortar Owner

Tony Marino

  • Age: 45
  • Profession: Owner of "Tony's Pizzeria"
  • Technology: iPhone 14 Pro, HP EliteBook 840 G11 laptop, and HP EliteOne 870 G9 desktop all running Firefox
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: Tony is motivated by the desire to grow his business and maintain long-term success. Being engaged in his community and supporting local events is a priority. Tony want his customers to be happy and is driven by positive feedback.
  • Frustrations and Needs: Tony struggles with the visibility of his restaurant on larger sites that prioritize larger franchises and national chains. Tony finds it difficult to keep up with marketing. He also has limited time to spend on things outside of managing the restaurant.
  • Goals: Tony wants to increase the visibility of his pizzeria to a larger customer base. He wants prospective customers to have easy access to his menu. Tony wants people looking for local businesses to be able to easily find his restaurant.
  • User Story: "As an owner of a restaurant, it's important to me that people know when I'm going to be open."

User Case

Tony has seen a surge in business recently and has decided to extend his hours. He visits his profile to update the pizzeria's business hours.

Pre-condition: Tony navigates to the Team Eats website and logs into his business profile.

Post-condition: Tony successfully updates the hours that his pizzeria is open.

Interaction Flow

  1. Tony clicks on his business profile.
  2. Team Eats website loads the page for Tony's Pizzeria.
  3. Tony clicks on the edit button.
  4. Team Eats website goes into the editing mode.
  5. Tony clicks the text box that lists his hours.
  6. Team Eats website focuses on the text box and allows text entry.
  7. Tony types out his updated business hours and clicks the button to save changes.
  8. Team Eats website updates the saved information in the database to show on Tony's Pizzeria's page.
