Ricky moves physically all day in his line of work, his tools are heavy that need to be carried to his work site. Ricky works long hours to update and remodel various industrial kitchens in town. After work He doesn't want to use his mind or research any restaurant, He would like a quick search for a well established restaurant that he has not been to or visited in a long time.

Frustrations and Needs:

Frustrations and Needs: Ricky works all day in construction on kitchens for the purpose of it to stay up to date so that it can facilitate it's amazing restaurants. When Ricky is off work, he is raising ten kids with his wife. His wife demands to look after the kids when he is home so she can take a break, he is so depleted from back breaking work that he cannot follow 3 instruction bullet points on a microwavable meal for himself. Ricky wants to have a few taps on his smartphone where he is rewarded to have a chance to take his family to dine in an amazing restaurant with fine cuisine that he has not experienced or not has been to in awhile.

Goals:

Ricky hopes that he has the ability to find an immediate restaurant through his smart phone where he can take his big family to dine in and fill their stomach with a satisfying meal.
